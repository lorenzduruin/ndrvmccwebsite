Vission

ND-RVMCC envision a dynamic learning community that empowers Ignacian Mariam leaders for inner and social transformation for the common good.

Mission

ND-RVMCC commits itself to: Develop Ignacian Mariam leaders who are witness of faith, excellence and services, Intensify instruction by pursuing innovative programs that employ relevant educational strategies for technological advancement and stewarship of creation; and enhance linkages to facilites instruction, research, and community involment to enhance to quality of life.
