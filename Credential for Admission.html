Credentials for Admission and Re-Admission

Incomming Freshmen
  • Entrance Test Result
  • Form 138
  • Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
  • Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 3 pcs. 2x2 I.D. Picture
  • 1 pc. Long Brown Craft Envelope
  • Mailing Envelope and stamp (Official RVM short envelope)
  • Marriage Certificate (for married)
Transferees: Transferee students are those who wish to enroll after having been enrolled in college at another institution. Former college students returning to ND-RVMCC after having enrolled in other institution are also considered transferees. They are required to submit the following credentials.
  • Entrance Test Result
  • Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
  • Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Transcript of Records of the school last attend
  • 3 pcs. 2x2 I.D. Picture
  • 1 pc. Long Brown Craft Envelope
  • Marriage Certificate (for married)

Basic Education Department

Kindergarden
Complete Elementary and High School Education

Admission Requirements:

  • Junior Kinder - The child should be at least 4 years old by the current school year
  • Grade One - The child should be at least 6 years old by the current school year
  • Have taken and passed the Placement Test and secure permit to enroll the Guidance Office.
  • Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
  • Birth Certificate (for Catholics) - Photocopy
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID Picture

Transferees:

  • Report card with satisfactory rating in all subjects
  • Have taken and passed the Placement Test and secure permit to enroll from the Guidance Office
  • Birth Certificate (SECPA from NSO)
  • Baptismal Certificate (for Catholics)
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID Picture
